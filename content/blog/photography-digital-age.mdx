# Markdown Syntax Guide

## Headings
# Heading 1
## Heading 2
### Heading 3
#### Heading 4
##### Heading 5
###### Heading 6

---

## Text Formatting
- **Bold**: `**Bold**` → **Bold**
- *Italic*: `*Italic*` → *Italic*
- ***Bold & Italic***: `***Bold & Italic***` → ***Bold & Italic***
- ~~Strikethrough~~: `~~Strikethrough~~` → ~~Strikethrough~~
- Inline code: `` `Inline code` `` → `Inline code`

---

### Unordered List

### Ordered List
